Data Policy

This policy describes information regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of user's (also "you") personal information when you use Witty Wallet's (also "us", "we", or "our") service. These policies are subject to change at any given time without notice.

I. What Information Do We Collect?

To provide Witty Wallet Services, we need to collect and process information about our users. This would include:

  • Information and Content You Provide

    Witty Wallet collects the content such as account information, budgets, transactions and other information you provide when you use Witty Wallet. We collect these information for the purposes stated in section II of this document.

  • Application Usage

    • We collect information about how users use our service. These information may include, but is not limited to, features you use, the time and frequency of your usage, and the last time you accessed the application.

II. How Do We Use Data We Collect?

Witty Wallet use the information collected as described bellow:

  • Providing Content and Personaliztion

    Witty Wallet use the information users provide to personalize features and content for each user; this may include, but is not limited to, customized components in the UI, reminders, and notifications. We also use your information to provide content and features such as budget forecasts.

  • Updates and Improvements

    We also use information from users to improve our service. Information can be used to develop new features and functions or improve existing ones.

  • Safety and Security

    We use information you provide to verify accounts and activity, maintain the integrity of our application and promote the security of Witty Wallet's users' accounts.

  • Reaching our Users

    We use the information we haver to communicate with you about our services. We also use the information you provide in order to respond to your queries when you contact us.
